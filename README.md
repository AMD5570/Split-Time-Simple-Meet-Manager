# [SplitTime - A Simple Swim Meet Manager](https://ff4.student.rit.edu/austin/Split-Time-Simple-Meet-Manager/)

SplitTime is an easy-to-use swim meet managing tool where you can track events, swimmers swimming those events, and times.
Think of it as a free, online alternative to programs such as Hy-Tek.

## Functionalities

You can create meets, events, and swimmers to swim in those events. Swimmers can have both a seed time and an actual time,
which can be added after inserting a swimmer.

You can add as many events and heats as you want!

If you need to remove a swimmer, event, or an entire meet you can!

All meets, events, and swimmers get saved locally to your device.

## Saving & Exporting

Once you're done creating a meet, you can save it and give it a name. You can also export your meet as a PDF or CVS file to
share it elsewhere.
